5 reasons to play League of Legends


The game is constantly updating!

With league constantly updating chapions, adding new champions, 3 different game modes and a rotational game mode the game always feels fresh as it updates every 2 weeks.

There's over 100 champions in the game!

With there being 154 champs there's always going to be one that suits your playstyle you want to be a tank? we got you fam, wanna not even play the game supports right there, want to feel like the most important player in the game whether you play good or not mid lane is all yours.


The Game modes!

As mentioned on the first point there are multiple games modes constantly rotating and new game modes being added keeping a good amount of variety in the game!


Massive player base!

League of legends is a game that been going on for longer than 10 years! With around 115 million users online you will always be able to get into a game and it also allows you to play with friends in a squad. This game might outlast us all!...


The more you level in game the better the rewards!

The more you level in game the more rewards you can get in the game, the higher level you are the higher quality the rewards you get are, you will get champion capsuls which allow you to get the champion you want for less in game currency(called blue essence) or you can disenchant for more blue essence to get the champion you want.
